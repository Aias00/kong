A train is a mode of transportation that typically runs on tracks and consists of a series of connected vehicles, called cars or carriages. Trains can transport passengers, cargo, or a combination of both. They are powered by various methods, including steam, diesel, and electricity. \n\nPassenger trains are designed to carry people and typically offer various amenities such as seating, restrooms, and sometimes dining or sleeping facilities.
